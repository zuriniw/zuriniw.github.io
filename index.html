I am interested in building new infrastructures to expand the affordances of everyday environments and to support meaningful interactions in a intelligence-infused reality.

Here are my interested research space:

<1> INTEGRATION: Infusing intelligence into the environment with sensitivity to human presence and behaviors, surfacing physically grounded, task-callable affordances.

<2> INTERACTIVITY: Turning those affordances into contextually usable, unobtrusive infrastructure by orchestrating proactivity, timing, and modalities of interaction.

Within this framing, my master's research is centered around proactive agents in everyday physical environments that present unique challenges and opportunities. We conducted empirical studies to understand human perception of them across tasks, context, proactivity types & levels, and form factors (submitted to CHI 2026), and are building technical systems that support unobtrusive interaction (targeting at UIST 2026). In my future PhD research, I am open to adding new layers to this space.

I pursue doing researches that can persist as AI evolves, not by keeping up with any specific model but by exploring retainable knowledge and the lasting potentials that emerge from them.
