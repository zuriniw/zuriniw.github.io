I am interested in building new intelligent infrastructures for everyday environments, aiming to enhance human ability and cognition and to support meaningful interaction in an intelligence-infused reality.

The figure above defines my interested research space and two possible directions for future exploration:

[1] AFFORDANCES: Apply intelligence to the physical world, from object level to room level, to give things new abilities. These abilities have the potential to reshape how the world is perceived and acted upon.

[2] INTERACTIVITY: Bring embedded intelligence to people with the right timing, initiative, and modality. This fosters unobtrusive and adaptive collaboration and communication.

I pursue research that can persist as AI models evolve—not by keeping up with one specific model, but by exploring retainable knowledge and lasting potential that emerge from them.
